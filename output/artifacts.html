Structures: Resource Profiles

These define constraints on FHIR resources for systems conforming to this implementation guide

Disease Management Plan

The PlanDefinition for a rare conditions refers to PlanDefinitions of other diseases, i.e. Shwachman-Diamond Syndrome is typically associated with Neutropenia. The disease plan refers to medication, lab tests and other items for management of the disease.
Lab Test Activity

A Disease is associated with Symptoms, which may be abnormal lab values, represented by LOINC codes.
Lab Test Observation

A Disease is associated with Symptoms, which may be abnormal lab values, represented by LOINC codes.
Medication Administration Activity

A Disease is treated with medication.
Rare Condition Management Plan

The PlanDefinition for a rare conditions refers to PlanDefinitions of other diseases, i.e. Shwachman-Diamond Syndrome is typically associated with Neutropenia. A code from Orphanet is required.
